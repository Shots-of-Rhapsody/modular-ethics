Radical transparency in our funding

Every contribution to Ethics Testbed is visible, accountable, and purposeful. Donations are treated as real fuel that keeps the project alive and evolving.

Public ledger on GitHub

All incoming funds and outgoing expenses are tracked openly in this repository. You can see where funds come from, how they are allocated, and what they produce.

Open discussions

Decisions about resource use are debated in public threads. Nothing is hidden behind closed doors. The community helps shape priorities and spending.

Traceable outcomes

For each expense, we connect results to the investment, whether it is new research, improved tools, or expanded community features.

How this works in practice
Each funded task is linked to a GitHub Issue or Discussion with acceptance criteria and artifacts. When complete, results are posted and cross-linked to the ledger entry for verification.

This structure guarantees that resources are not misused or quietly diverted. They are openly tracked and directly reinvested into the growth and evolution of the project. By funding Ethics Testbed, you are not only donating. You are joining a living record of collaborative progress, where transparency is part of the code.
