Who I Am

My name’s Tai Song. I build systems. In trade and tech, I set up the structures behind how things move: products, information, decisions. I work on global markets, AI ethics, ESG contracts, and long-term planning. I rely on models, mental frameworks, and spotting patterns. I try to make things clear without locking them down.

But I’ve always had two tracks going at once. There’s the outside world I work in, and the internal one I’ve explored through stories. They’re usually grounded in dynasties, cultivation, and old myths. My writing digs into power, change, and how hard it is to evolve without coming apart.

Different tools, same mission. Figure out how things work, and shift them deliberately. One path helps me handle the world I’m in. The other keeps pushing me to ask what kind of world we should actually build.

What I Build

My work is about risk and design. It's about how to face what we don’t see coming and plan for what comes next.

I build systems that scan for early signals, track weird patterns, and flag potential threats before they show up at the front door. I work on ethics in AI, trying to keep automation tied to real accountability. I write contracts that translate ESG goals into actions people can actually track. I help shape digital infrastructure that can take a hit, follow the rules, and still move fast.

None of this is static. These systems are built to learn and adjust as the world changes. I pull from a lot of fields: geopolitics, climate shifts, behavioral science, data law. When you line them up right, they don’t just reduce risk. They create alignment. They help people move. They make resilience something you can actually build into the system. Every project is a custom setup. It’s not just about structure. It’s about intent. And making sure it holds when pressure hits.

What I Write

Alongside my strategy work, I write speculative fiction influenced by Xianxia, dynastic systems, and philosophy. It’s not a break from what I do. It’s another way of studying how systems shift, hold together, or fall apart. The stories look at the tension between personal drive and inherited structure. They ask what power costs, how people change, and what legacy really means. The worlds I build treat belief like architecture and culture like the ground it stands on.

Every story is an experiment. A sealed system. A world with its own rules, with characters trying to bend or break them. Fiction gives me room to explore what data can’t reach: moral consequences, long memory, and the inner logic that shapes how civilizations grow or collapse.

How I Work

I work with people who operate where systems are stressed or shifting. That might be where AI and ethics collide, where trade runs into climate limits, or where governance has to meet the power of narrative.

Whether the job is navigating regulation, setting up accountability, or building fictional worlds that actually make sense, I bring a mix of structure and imagination. I move between domains, but I stay grounded in the details. Every project adapts to its environment. But the core goals don’t change. Make things align. Make them hold up under pressure. And do it at scale.

Work With Me

This site opens two directions. One is focused on strategy: consulting in AI ethics, foresight, ESG systems, and digital policy. The other dives into storytelling: speculative fiction, worldbuilding, and myth-driven narratives.

Both tracks are about building and change. Start from wherever you are. Work the structure. Shape what comes next.
