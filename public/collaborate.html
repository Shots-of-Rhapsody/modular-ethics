Radical transparency in our funding

Every contribution to Ethics Testbed is visible, accountable, and purposeful. Donations are treated as real fuel that keeps the project alive and evolving.

Public ledger on GitHub

All incoming funds and outgoing expenses are tracked openly. You can see where funds come from, how they are allocated, and what they produce.

Open discussions

Decisions about resource use are debated in public threads. Nothing is hidden behind closed doors. The community helps shape priorities and spending.

Traceable outcomes

Each funded task links to a GitHub Issue or Discussion with acceptance criteria and artifacts. Completed work is cross-referenced so anyone can audit the trail.

How this works in practice
Every expense is tied to a specific unit of progress (commit, artifact, or publication). The mapping stays readable and verifiable by outsiders.

This structure keeps resources openly tracked and directly reinvested into the project. By funding Ethics Testbed, you join a living record of collaborative progress where transparency is part of the code.
