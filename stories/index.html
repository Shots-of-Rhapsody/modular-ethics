Stories

Fiction and non-fiction, both tracing the lines where systems meet human lives.

Fiction

Science meets sorrow, memory fades & futures fracture. The edge between invention & consequence, searching for what we lose in what we make. Quiet apocalypses, slow transformations & fragile things we try to hold onto before they disappear.

Non-Fiction

Power hums beneath policy, inequality bleeds through markets. Systems shape us, quietly, as empires shift and vanish. Watching what breaks, what endures, and what slips through the cracks when the world decides who gets what and why.
